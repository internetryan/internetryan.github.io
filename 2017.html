2017, in review

This year is best summed up by the day I, after an incredibly frustrating and heartbreaking week, was openly yelling at a realtor over the phone in front of an apartment building. While I was misdirecting my anger at this poor man, a guy walked by carrying groceries. He stopped suddenly, spun on his heels, reached into one of the bags, and handed me a beer. I don’t drink so I returned the beer, hung up the phone, and just burst out laughing at how absurd I must have looked in that moment. Thankfully, the next day I finally found an apartment (sourced by a different realtor, of course). There’s a lot to be angry about these days, but there’s also the occasional stranger on the sidewalk offering up a beer, so I think we’ll be okay.
